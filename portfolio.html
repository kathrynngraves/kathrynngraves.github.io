Yale University 3-Minute Thesis

The Yale “3-Minute Thesis Competition” challenges Ph.D. students to concisely share their research with a non-specialist audience. In 2023 I won the Social Sciences 3MT with this presentation of my research.





Yale Research Psychology Bootcamp

The Yale Psychology Department Committee on Diversity and Inclusion aims to create a diverse and inclusive environment for faculty, students, and staff, and for the STEM community more broadly. As co-chair of this committee, I lead organization of the inaugural, 2nd annual, and 3rd annual 3 hour Psychology "boot camp" for over 2000 primarily underrepresented minority undergraduate and post-graduate attendees. Video from these events, as well as resources for pursuing a career in psychology, are permanently hosted on the Yale Psychology website.

https://psychology.yale.edu/graduate/admissions/psychology-graduate-school-bootcamp
